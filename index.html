WHAT IS CODE{X}??

Code{x} is a vibrant coding club dedicated to fostering a passion for programming among enthusiasts of all levels. Through workshops, projects, and collaborative sessions, members explore diverse coding languages and technologies. With a supportive community and engaging activities, Code{x} empowers individuals to unleash their creativity and advance their coding skills.
